Transform your everyday shower into a spa-like escape with our Upfront Cosmetics Shower Steamer. Perfect for those who prefer showers over baths, this aromatic steam-activated tablet infuses your bathroom with luxurious, therapeutic essential oils — turning even the quickest rinse into a moment of calm.

Simply place the steamer on the floor of your shower, away from direct water flow, and let the gentle splash of droplets release its soothing fragrance. As it dissolves, it fills the air with an immersive scent experience designed to relax the mind, uplift your mood, and elevate your entire routine.

Why You’ll Love It

  • Aromatherapy, simplified: Enjoy the benefits of essential oils without the need for a bath.

  • Effortless self-care: One tablet delivers a rich, spa-worthy scent experience.

  • Cleaner beauty: Made with simple, premium ingredients — always vegan and cruelty-free.

  • Perfect for gifting: A small luxury that brings joy to any shower.

Make your daily ritual something to look forward to. Breathe in, unwind, and let your shower become your sanctuary.

Perfect for bedtime or stuffy-nose mornings.
